Definition / Explanation:

The ASK price is the price at which you can buy.

The BID price is the price at which you can sell.

There’s always a small difference between these prices called the spread.

When placing a BUY trade, it opens at the ASK price and closes at the BID price.

When placing a SELL trade, it opens at the BID price and closes at the ASK price.
This explains why trades often execute at a price different from the one displayed on the chart, which usually shows only one of these prices (often the BID).
